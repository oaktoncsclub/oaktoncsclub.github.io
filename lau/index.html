The Lau Board

What is Lau Board?

Originally the Lau Board was an iOS soundboard app designed as a tribute to Mr. Lau and his amazing quotes written by Ben Cullen. Unfortunately, the App Store review team decided to reject the app because it is not "useful" enough, i.e. only Mr. Lau's students would find it interesting. But we all know, these inspiring quotes are for everyone.
